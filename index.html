$$u(x) = \frac{x}{10}$$

Answer Exercise 1a

The expected value of the prospect is EV=0.2*40+0.6*50+0.2*30=44 and the utility given the formula
U(x)=∑p * u
is 4.4.
The certainty equivalent (CE) is calculated by determining the value of x for which an individual is indifferent of receiving the prospect or a certain amount. In this case, since utility is given by
$$U(x)=\frac{x}{10}$$
the CE is calculated as follows:


$$U(x)= \frac{x}{10} =4.4$$

x = 4.4 * 10 = 44 = CE
